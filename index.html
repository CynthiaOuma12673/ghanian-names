GHANIAN AKAN NAMES

This is an application that takes user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in the Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names for male and female.

Male

  • Sunday:Kwasi
  • Monday:Kwadwo
  • Tuesday:Kwabena
  • Wednesday:Kwaku
  • Thursday:Yaw
  • Friday:Kofi
  • Saturday:Kwame

Female

  • Sunday:Akosua
  • Monday:Adwoa
  • Tuesday:Abenaa
  • Wednesday:Akua
  • Thursday:Yaa
  • Friday:Afua
  • Saturday:Ama

Most people do know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born. Luckily for us, there are ways to calculate the day of the week from a specific date, below is a way in which one can know their ghanian names from date of birth.
